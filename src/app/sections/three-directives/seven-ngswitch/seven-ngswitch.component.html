7. The ngSwitch Directive

Do you prefer to travel by train, plane or car?

Plane

It's definitely the fastest way. It is very interesting to fly among the clouds in the sky and a great opportunity to take nice pictures.

Train

You can enjoy beautiful landscapes from a very close distance. You don't have to drive or look at any signs. Simply relax and enjoy the ride.

Car

Freedom and flexibility to go wherever you want, whenever you want and do as many stops as you like.
